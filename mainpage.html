Medsicle.
Medicine, streamlined.

Welcome to innovation in medicine.

The US ranks last place among the wealthiest nations in the world in terms of healthcare. One of the primary reasons for this is the lack of efficiency in the system. Although the US spends the most (and by a large margin) on healthcare compared to the rest of the world, it still has the worst quality. A lack of a streamlined medical system does not help this problem. Medsicle is a site created by 4 high school students. We wanted to make a project that impacted our community. Medsicle streamlines mediacal information for the user, including their medical information, records, possible doctors and professionals, and even a chat system providing a connection between the user and the professional.
